Manage your immigration application online

Track your application status, upload supporting documents, make secure payments, and communicate with our support team — all from one secure portal.

Create an account Sign in to your account

Track your application

Follow your application from Draft through to a final decision using a clear, step-by-step progress tracker.

Upload documents securely

Submit your passport, identification, photos, and supporting documents in PDF, JPG, or PNG format, with full upload history.

Pay application fees

Pay by Visa, Mastercard, American Express, bank transfer, or arrange for a representative to pay on your behalf.

Get real-time notifications

Receive updates when documents are reviewed, payments are received, or a decision is issued on your application.

Your unique client identifiers

Every applicant receives a unique Client Identifier (UCI) and GC Reference Number, used to identify your file throughout the process.

Contact support

Send secure messages to our support team directly from your dashboard and receive responses without leaving the portal.

Ready to begin?

New applicants can create a secure account in a few minutes. You will need a digital copy of your passport, a passport-style photo, and any supporting documents for your application.
